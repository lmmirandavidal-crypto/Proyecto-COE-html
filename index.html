Project: COE Management System

Transforming Auditing into Daily Operational Excellence

Presented by: Lucas Miranda Vidal

Date: November 2025

The Current Challenge

Auditing as an Event, Not a Habit

Reactive & High-Effort Preparation

Auditing consumes hours of preparation, diverting focus from daily operations.

Lack of Real-Time Visibility

Management lacks a consolidated view of compliance status, discovering gaps too late.

Risk of Inconsistencies

Teams manage evidence in isolation, creating information silos.

"Excellence" as an Annual Goal

Quality is measured in peaks of effort, not as a daily cultural habit.

Our Solution

The Integrated COE Management System

We translated 59 audit points and 284 verification questions into a proactive, simple, and automated tool.

Audit Framework
COE SYSTEM
  • Daily Tasks
  • Dashboards
  • Reports

Automates Execution

Converts audit theory into daily tasks assigned to the correct role.

Centralizes Management

Acts as the "single source of truth" for all evidence and action plans.

Visualizes Performance

Offers a 360° view of excellence status through intelligent dashboards.

Strategic Benefits

A Clear Return on Investment (ROI)

1. From Reaction to Prevention

Ensures daily compliance and minimizes audit findings.

2. Data-Driven Governance

Enables informed decision-making and real-time performance comparison.

3. Operational Efficiency

Drastic reduction in man-hours for audit preparation (2 hours x day x role estimated during 3 months of preparation). Teams focus on excellence day-to-day.

4. Sustainable Quality Culture

Fosters a culture where excellence is the starting point of every day.
